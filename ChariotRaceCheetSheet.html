Your horses and chariot will be treated as one single entity. The total HP is just the sum of the chariot's HP and 2 of your horse's HP. The maximum movespeed of your cart is the speed of the chariot plus half the speed of one of your horses. This game will be ignoring the dash action for the horses for reasons explained later.
